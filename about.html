About EduAdmin Pro

We're on a mission to simplify school administration and amplify education for schools worldwide.

Our Story

EduAdmin Pro was founded in 2018 by educators who experienced firsthand the challenges of managing a school with outdated, fragmented systems.

After spending countless hours on paperwork, attendance tracking, and manual data entry, we knew there had to be a better way. We set out to create a platform that would free up educators to focus on what really matters: teaching and supporting students.

Today, EduAdmin Pro serves over 2,500 schools worldwide, helping administrators save 10+ hours per week and improving communication with parents and students.
